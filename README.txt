Dual-mode Tic-Tac-Toe web app - supports two modes:

1) 2 Players (Local): Two people play on same device, taking turns by clicking cells.
2) 1 Player (You vs AI): Play against computer. Choose whether you are X or O and AI difficulty (Unbeatable uses Minimax, Easy uses random moves).

Files:
- index.html
- styles.css
- script.js

Open index.html in a browser to play. Restart keeps scores; refresh page to fully reset scores.
